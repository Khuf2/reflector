The Reflector Attribute Journal helps you collect information with regard to health and wellbeing over an extended period of time. With this data, you can observe relationships between attributes using your journal entries and dynamic graphing tools. Sign up and start tracking your entries in a few clicks.
*Not all features are available on mobile, wide display recommended.*

Each day, record a score for each of the following metrics:

(1-10)

Satisfaction
  • - Rating of daily satisfaction
  • - Most general metric for ranking the quality of a day

(1-10)

Stress
  • - Rating of daily stress
  • - May have an inverse relationship with satisfaction
  • - Magnitude of stress rating depends on the intensity of immediate or foreseen stressors

(1-10)

Exercise
  • - Rating of daily physical activity or fitness
  • - Covers a wide breadth of activities
  • - Magnitude of exercise rating depends on the duration and intensity of the activity

(1-10)

Nutrition
  • - Rating of daily diet
  • - Magnitude of nutrition rating may depend on nutritional quality or simply eating well throughout the day, depending on your goals

(1-10)

Productivity
  • - Rating of daily work done or tasks completed
  • - For academic, workplace or ambitious efforts
  • - Focus and effectiveness
  • - Magnitude of productivity rating depends on the intensity of the work

(1-10)

Social
  • - Rating of daily social activity
  • - From familial, platonic, or intimate relationships
  • - Magnitude of social rating depends on the duration and setting of the interaction

(1-10)

Sleep
  • - Rating of daily sleep quality
  • - Adherence to sleep schedule or supplemental naps may help this score
  • - Magnitude of sleep rating depends on the duration and quality of the sleep
Extra
  • - No accompanying rating
  • - Space to add additional information that may not adhere to a particular category
  • - Notes, goals experiences, or upcoming things to look out for can go here
